About Us

Best Logistics Provider In Nepal

“Our core aphorism is the contentment of the client & their value of the time with reliability and security.”
With this theme, Hansa Logistics Nepal began operations in June 2004 with the purpose of creating an ocean brokerage, international freight forwarding based on knowledge and experience, which could provide exporters as well as importers with a choice of an best quality services. We are a company based on Nepal providing all the logistics services authorized under legal laws of Government of Nepal as its associates. Hansa has been providing modernized and trusted services with almost two decades experiences from their experts using the modern techniques, amenities and solutions. We pledge our customer with secure & optimal passage of precious freight to different sectors as per client desires.

Discover What We Do & Offer To Our Clients

Air Freight Forwarding

Air freight plays a key role in the success of your business and strive to provide you with reliable and cost-efficient air freight options. So, when it comes to air freight transportation, Hansa logistics has the right solution for you.

Ocean Freight Forwarding

We cover the entire fields of ocean products. In addition to standard products like FCL and LCL.Through a robust network of connections with sea carriers we are able to deliver a fast, cost-efficient and reliable ocean freight services.

Road Freight Forwarding

Road freight service runs across a global platform manned by a team of experts uniquely skilled at overseeing overland operations, including customs requirements. We can provide efficient solution for road freight transportation.

Custom Brokerage

Our team of experts are completely abreast with current customs regulations and schedules. Our years of experience ensure that we are up-to-speed on every amendment, every change and every development in the law of the land and in its administrative regulations. When it comes to moving your products across international borders, we provide you with a smooth and hassle-free experience.

Warehousing & Distribution

With the on going need of cost efficient and reliable solutions for warehousing, Hansa logistics manages warehouse based on the need of our valued customers with the most reliable and effective way. We follow best practices to manage your inventories and ensure timely deliveries and distribution.

We analyze information to identify and solve any logistics and supply chain operation problems for our clients. We provide ideas and help you regarding logistics documentation.
